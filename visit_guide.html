Visit guide

Fountain

This is a show to see! The largest fountain system in the world with over 6,000 lights and the best of Arabic and world music. Can you believe it has cost over $200 mln to create? The fountain sits proudly in front of the Dubai Mall - definitely inviting to take a peek into this shopping kingdom! This is a landmark to see.

Burj Khalifa

Another world record breaking landmark to see! It's the highest skyscraper in the world, measuring over 829 meters high. Yes, you can get all the way to the tops to see the spectacular views, thanks to 57 operating elevators it's really easy to do. Hopefully you're not scared of heights.

Marina

Have you ever seen a man-made waterfront? With a yacht club, boutiques, gyms and amazing wildlife, including sharks and whales you will be amazed with this lively new part of Dubai.

Mall

It's also known as home to the Dubai shopping festival. Apart from boutiques and fine dinig, you can find there aqarium and uderwater zoo and an ice rink. Oh, and it's the largest shopping mall in the world too!

Burj Al Arab

Have you ever heard of a 7 star hotel? This is the place! The most luxurious hotel in the world. If you can't afford to get inside just yet, have a good look from outside, it's been acclaimed as one of the most inspiring buildings of the world.

Desert

Belly dancing, camel riding, dune bashing, sand ski, sunset photography, shisha, sleeping under the stars. Where can you combine all of these? On a trip to the Sahara desert, just out of Dubai city. That would defintiely be an unforgetable adventure!

Beaches

So you've seen all the sights and could so with some down time? Hitting the beach is definitely a thing to do in Dubai. It's perefectly fine to wear swimwear on the beaches so get your bikins ready. To make you even more comfortable, you can go to women and family-only beaches. Feel free to play beach games, have a picinc or just have a stroll in this beautiful scenery.

Garden

If you love nature, this is the place to visit! The Miracle Garden is the largest natural flower garden in the world. There is also a Butterfly and Aromatic Gardens. Don't miss it!
